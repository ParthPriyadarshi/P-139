Ping-Pong

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. There should be a distance of approximately 3 to 4 feet.
  3. Move your right wrist in ascending and descending manner.
  4. , A red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  5. Press the Play Button to start the Game.
  6. The Red Paddle is your Paddle from which you are going to play.
